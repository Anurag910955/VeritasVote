About VeritasVote

VeritasVote is a transparent and secure online voting system designed to ensure fairness and integrity in elections. Leveraging modern web technologies like React.js, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB, it provides a reliable platform where voters can confidently cast their votes without concerns of manipulation or fraud.

The system is built with user-friendly interfaces, making it accessible to voters of all backgrounds. Key features include voter authentication, real-time vote tallying, and detailed results analysis, ensuring every vote counts and is properly recorded.

Our mission is to modernize the electoral process by combining technology and trust. VeritasVote not only simplifies the voting experience but also enhances security, allowing institutions to host elections with complete transparency.
